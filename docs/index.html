I am 25 years old living in Chatsworth, California. I work on computer vision and robotics algorithms. I love math, programming, tennis and BJJ (in that order). I write some technical things, but mainly just rant about the world here. I was recently laid off and am looking for work in computer vision & robotics algorithm research.

You may have stumbled upon this blog before, and noticed that I deleted all of my old posts. It’s because I feel like the writing quality was sloppy and I don’t want to keep them up, so it’s better to just delete them and start fresh.

One of my proudest moments is getting a 35 on the math ACT, which isn’t really very impressive, thousands of people get a perfect 36. But I’m allowed to brag about the things I’m proud of. On the weekends, I usually do AMC 10 problems. I can keep up until about problem 15. On a good day without careless errors, that enough to qualify for AIME. The problem is that I was 8 years too late. Regardless, math is fun. Also I am a programming god, but I don’t have any results that prove it yet.
